# Submitting a Project

CS-Arena lets you showcase your CS projects to the world. Here's how to get your project listed.

## Requirements

Before submitting, make sure your project has:

- ✅ A public **GitHub repository**
- ✅ A clear **title** (3–100 characters)
- ✅ A **description** (20–500 characters)
- ✅ At least **one tech stack** tag
- ✅ A **cover image** URL

## How to Submit

1. Click **"Create Project"** in the navbar
2. Fill in the project details form
3. Add your GitHub repository link
4. Select your tech stack tags
5. Write your project pitch in the markdown editor
6. Click **"Submit"**

> Your project will appear instantly on the Explore page after submission.

## Managing Your Project

After submitting, you can view your project from your **Profile page**. From there you can:

- View how many people have seen your project
- See your upvote count
- Check if contributors have reached out

## Looking for Contributors?

Toggle the **"Looking for Contributors"** switch when submitting. Your project will appear on the **Find Developers** page, where other developers can discover and join your team.
