# Find Developers

The **Find Developers** page is where collaboration happens. Browse projects actively looking for contributors and join a team.

## How It Works

Projects that have enabled the **"Looking for Contributors"** flag appear here. You can filter by tech stack to find projects that match your skills.

## Finding the Right Project

Use the filters at the top of the page to narrow down by:

- **Tech Stack** — Filter by Next.js, React, Node.js, Python, Java, and more
- **Sort** — Sort by newest, most upvoted, or most viewed

## Joining a Project

1. Browse the **Find Developers** page
2. Click **"Details"** on a project that interests you
3. Visit the project's **GitHub repository**
4. Open an **issue** or **pull request** to introduce yourself

## Listing Your Project

When creating or editing your project, enable the **"Looking for Contributors"** toggle. Your project will automatically appear on this page.

> **Tip:** Write a clear pitch explaining what kind of help you need. It significantly increases your chances of finding great contributors.
